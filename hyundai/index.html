FAQ

What is the Hyundai Metamobility universe?
Hyundai Metamobility universe is a world where the boundaries between virtual and reality, time and space have disappeared.
It is the universe of Hyundai brand NFT, a world where you can freely move beyond time and space with
Hyundai Motor Company’s Metamobility.

At CES 2022, Hyundai Motor Company has introduced the concept of ‘Metamobility,’
which will expand human reach to ultimate freedom of mobility.
We aim to bring that vision to life through the new world of ‘Hyundai Metamobility universe’.

Starting with the official NFT launch on May 9th, 2022,
Hyundai NFT project will provide the Hyundai brand experience in the metaverse.
What is an NFT?
NFT is an abbreviation for Non-Fungible Token. It is a cryptographic asset that is stored on a blockchain,
but different from other digital assets as it has a unique identification code which makes it non-interchangeable.
What is minting?
Minting is the act of verifying data on the blockchain and uniquely publishing it on a new block.
“NFT minting” basically means publishing NFT digital assets such as art and other creative content.
When will the minting take place?
When will the minting take place? Hyundai Metamobility universe’s first official minting is scheduled for May 9th(Pre-sale for whitelist) and May 10th(General sale), 2022.
Hyundai’s first official NFT, Shooting star NFT, will be issued at this time.
What is Shooting star NFT?
The first NFT presented in Hyundai Metamobility universe :
Hyundai’s first official NFT collection consists of Shooting star NFTs that fell on Earth, bursting with infinite kinetic energy.

They are the first tickets to enter Hyundai Metamobility universe, the driving force to open the possibilities of future mobility.
In late May, Shooting star NFTs will be automatically converted into Metamobility NFTs with unique individual designs in a holder’s wallet.

The reveal date will be updated soon.
How do I purchase a Shooting star NFT?
All shooting star NFTs have now transformed into MobED NFTs, and are available for purchase on the secondary market platforms such as Opensea.
What is a whitelist?
A whitelist is a list of pre-approved members for early access and guaranteed spots to specific mintings.

The whitelist for Shooting star NFT will be selected through a random draw before sale,
among the accounts of the most active engagement in the build of our community.
These accounts will be granted access to the pre-sale.
The mission for whitelist registration has already been announced, so please check out Hyundai NFT’s official Discord for details.
How much are the minting fees?
The minting fee for both pre-sale and general sale is 0.15 ETH each.
How many Shooting star NFTs will be available?
There will be 10,000 in total.
9,500 will be sold during the pre-sale and general sale.
The remaining 500 will be distributed through various routes such as airdrops, events, and other promotions, to increase asset value for this project.
What are the benefits of Shooting star NFTs?
Shooting star NFT is a rare limited edition collection of Hyundai Metamobility universe’s first ever NFT.

Further benefits : Such as airdrop, giveaway events, advertisement at a global landmark are planned to grow community .

Please check out our roadmap and the official Hyundai NFT Discord for more details and updates.
What is a Gas Fee?
When minting an NFT, the network usage fee issued in the process of exchanging data in the blockchain network is called “Gas Fee”. Shooting star NFT is minted on the Ethereum blockchain, so gas fees are charged as a price for using the Ethereum blockchain.

Gas fee can fluctuate as it depends on the environment of the Ethereum blockchain at the moment of minting. Users will need to pay for the gas fee,
since it is charged as a processing power provided by Ethereum blockchain network.
What is MobED NFT?
MobED is the first Metamobility NFT collection from the new world with infinite freedom of mobility : the Hyundai Metamobility Universe.
As of May 30th, 2022, MobED NFTs have been transformed from 10,000 shooting star NFTs.
All 10,000 NFTs were all created with unique characteristics and designs as generative art.
How do I purchase a MobED NFT?
MobED NFTs were originally sold at 0.15 ETH in a form of shooting stars. Its minting is now entirely completed, and are available on the secondary market platforms such as Opensea.
